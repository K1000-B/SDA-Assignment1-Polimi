\renewcommand{\parttitle}{Control Effectiveness Computation}
\section*{\parttitle}

The control effectiveness is defined as $E_C = \frac{L}{L_R}$. The total lift is obtained by spanwise integration:
\begin{align}
L = \int_0^b &L'(y)\,dy
= q c \left(
\int_0^b C_{L/\alpha}\,
\underbrace{\theta(y)}_{\frac{y}{b} \phi_\theta}
dy
+ \int_{b-b_a}^{b} C_{L/\beta}\,\beta\,dy
\right) \nonumber \\[8pt]
&\boxed{L = \underbrace{q c b_a C_{L/\beta}\,\beta}_{\displaystyle L_R\ \text{(Rigid wing)}}
\;+\;
\underbrace{q c \frac{b C_{L/\alpha}}{2}\,\phi_\theta}_{\displaystyle L_E\ \text{(Elastic torsion)}}}
\end{align}

\vspace{0.5cm}

We need to determine $\phi_\theta$. From the first question, we have :
\begin{equation}
\begin{aligned}
\left( \mathbf{K}_S - q \mathbf{K}_A \right)
\begin{Bmatrix}
\phi_w \\
\phi_\theta
\end{Bmatrix}
&= q \beta \mathbf{F}_a
\quad &
\text{where we define } \mathbf{K}_{\mathrm{TOT}} &= \mathbf{K}_S - q \mathbf{K}_A
\end{aligned}
\end{equation}

For $q < q_D$, $\mathbf{K}_{\mathrm{TOT}}$ is invertible:
\begin{equation}
\mathbf{K}_{\mathrm{TOT}}^{-1}
=
\frac{1}{\det(\mathbf{K}_{\mathrm{TOT}})}
\begin{bmatrix}
K_{\mathrm{TOT},22} & -K_{\mathrm{TOT},12} \\
-K_{\mathrm{TOT},21} & K_{\mathrm{TOT},11}
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation}

Where $\det(\mathbf{K}_{\mathrm{TOT}})$ is defined at equation \eqref{eq:det_KTOT}. Thus:
\begin{equation}
\begin{Bmatrix}
\phi_w \\
\phi_\theta
\end{Bmatrix}
=
q \beta \mathbf{K}_{\mathrm{TOT}}^{-1} \mathbf{F}_a
\end{equation}

The aileron influence load vector $\mathbf{F}_a$ is defined at \eqref{eq:Fa}. From matrix multiplication:
\begin{equation}
\phi_\theta
=
\frac{q \beta}{\det(\mathbf{K}_{\mathrm{TOT}})}
\left(
K_{\mathrm{TOT},11} F_{a2}
-
K_{\mathrm{TOT},21} F_{a1}
\right)
\end{equation}

\vspace{0.5cm}

Substituting into the lift expression:
\begin{equation}
L =q c \beta \left(
b_a C_{L/\beta}\,
+
 \frac{b C_{L/\alpha}}{2}
\cdot
\frac{q }{\det(\mathbf{K}_{\mathrm{TOT}})}
\left(
K_{\mathrm{TOT},11} F_{a2}
-
K_{\mathrm{TOT},21} F_{a1}
\right) \right)
\end{equation}

Dividing by $L_R$ gives the control effectiveness $E_C$:
\begin{equation}
\boxed{
E_C
=
1
+
q \cdot \frac{b}{b_a}\cdot \frac{C_{L/\alpha}}{C_{L/\beta}}
\cdot
\frac{1}{2 \det(\mathbf{K}_{\mathrm{TOT}})} \cdot 
\left(
K_{\mathrm{TOT},11} F_{a2}
-
K_{\mathrm{TOT},21} F_{a1}
\right)
}
\end{equation}

The expression of $E_C$ separates the rigid contribution from the aeroelastic correction.
At $q=0$, one recovers $E_C=1$ (rigid limit); as $q$ increases, aileron-induced torsion
modifies total lift. The correction is amplified as $\det(\mathbf{K}_{\mathrm{TOT}})\to 0$
(divergence), so $E_C$ may vanish before divergence -- defining the control reversal pressure.
